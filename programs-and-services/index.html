Educating.Empowering. Embracing.
Every woman carries great potential. In response to the unique needs of women experiencing homelessness we have developed a nine-week program with a focus on job-readiness, empowerment, support and health. Designed innovative and holistic approach ensures women break the cycle of homelessness for themselves, their children, and future generations.

LEADERSHIP ACADEMY

In our Leadership Academy, we introduce young women to purposeful exploration that is aligned with their passions and skillsets. We equip young women who have found their passion with the toolkit necessary to turn their interests into a career. We work with our incredible brand partners to provide support through entry-levels career opportunities.
  • Career Guidance
  • Access to Resources
  • Internship Placement
  • Career Development
  • Resume and Interview Preparedness Workshops

GIRLS EMPOWERMENT

Our Girls Empowerment program empowers teen girls to achieve their goals and take responsibility for their lives by learning self-directed skills so they can become strong confident young ladies. Girls will learn life skills that often take adults a lifetime to learn. We offer workshops and events for girls in 8th12th grades. Some of the expected results include attitude improvement, improved school performance and grades, better decision-making, improved relationships and conflict resolution, and confidence building.
  • Social Media
  • Body Image
  • Nutrition and Wellness, Friendships and Peer Communication Skills
  • Decision Making and Role Models
  • Mental and Emotional Wellbeing

SISTER CIRCLE: MENTAL HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

The Sister Circle program is a virtual space for young women and girls to discuss various topics pertaining to overall mental health and well-being. It includes monthly workshops and webinars facilitated by various licensed therapists.
  • Mindfulness
  • Resilience and Stress Reduction
  • Meditation
  • Mindful Eating and Nutrition
  • Self-Care
